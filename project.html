My Project-Restaurant Management System

I have done a mini-project on Restaurant management system. This project is based on database management system. It includes the management of HR, finance, inventory, security, bookings and reservation. It maintains records of staff, oreder,inventory and stock management. The project is insight into the design and implementaion of a Restaurant management system. The primary aim is to evaluate the need for a management perspective to better serve customers and improve outcomes to the development of buisness.
